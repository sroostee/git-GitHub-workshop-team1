First mash the cucumber and add salt. Leave aside for 20 min

You can also not add salt and squeeze juice out with a towel without waiting the 20 min.
Then mix in order 1 cup of yoghurt with two spoon fulls olive oil, one tea spoon of salt and a drop of lemon zest. 

After the 20 min are over, squeeze cucumber to remove juice and add it to the rest. 

Mix well, and your tzatziki is ready!

Good luck!
