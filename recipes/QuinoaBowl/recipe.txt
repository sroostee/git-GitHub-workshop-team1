👩‍🍳 Instructions
Cook the quinoa
Rinse quinoa, then cook it in water (bring to boil, then simmer ~15 minutes until fluffy).
Prepare the chicken
Rub with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
Cook the chicken
Grill or pan-cook for about 6–7 minutes per side until fully cooked. Let it rest, then slice.
Cook the vegetables
Steam or sauté broccoli and carrots until tender but still crisp.
Assemble the bowl
Add quinoa as the base, then top with chicken, vegetables, and avocado.
Finish
Drizzle with lemon juice and a little extra olive oil if desired.
