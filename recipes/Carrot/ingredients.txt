Ingredients

For the Cake:

Dry: 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour, 2 tsp baking powder, 1 tsp baking soda, ½ tsp salt, 1 ½ tsp cinnamon, 1 tsp ginger, ¼ tsp nutmeg, and ¼ tsp cloves.
Wet: 2 ½ cups brown sugar, ½ cup white sugar, 1 cup vegetable oil, 4 large eggs, ¾ cup unsweetened applesauce, and 1 tsp vanilla extract.
Add-ins: 2 cups freshly grated carrots and 1 cup chopped pecans or walnuts. 

For the Frosting:
16 oz brick-style cream cheese (softened), ½ cup unsalted butter (softened), 4 cups powdered sugar, and 1 ½ tsp vanilla extract.
