The Most Amazing Chocolate Cake – Instructions

Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease three 23 cm round cake pans with butter, then dust with flour and tap out the excess.
In a stand mixer, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Mix on low speed until blended.
Add eggs, buttermilk, warm water, oil, and vanilla extract. Beat on medium speed until smooth (about 1–2 minutes).
Add the chocolate chips (all of them!).
Divide the batter evenly between the three pans (about 700–750 ml batter per pan).
Bake for 30–35 minutes at 175°C, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
Let the cakes cool in the pans for 15 minutes, then turn them out onto wire racks and cool completely.
Frost with your preferred frosting and serve.



Chocolate Cream Cheese Buttercream Frosting – Instructions

In a large bowl, beat the butter and cream cheese until light and fluffy (using a hand mixer or stand mixer).
Add cocoa powder and vanilla extract. Beat until fully combined.
Gradually add powdered sugar (about 120 g at a time), beating well after each addition.
Add milk as needed (start with small amounts) until you reach a smooth, spreadable consistency.
The frosting should be thick and will firm up further if refrigerated.
