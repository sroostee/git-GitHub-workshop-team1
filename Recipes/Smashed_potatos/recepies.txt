1. Boil potatoes in salted water until fork-tender.
2. Drain and place on a baking tray.
3. Gently smash each potato with a glass or fork.
4. Brush with butter or olive oil.
5. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and parmesan if using.
6. Bake at 220°C for 25–35 minutes until golden and crispy.
7. Serve hot.

Great with roasted chicken or a fresh salad.
